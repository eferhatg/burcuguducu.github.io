about me

She was born in Samsun/Turkey in 1986. In her childhood, she drew the walls and the paper canvas whenever she found. She studied engineering and worked as an engineer for many years. Besides, she never stopped drawing and dreaming. Her daughter was born and Burcu started drawing children’s books for her. Burcu, who is a full-time illustrator, paints children’s books in her studio in Istanbul and feels very fortunate that she nurtures the imagination of children.
